The earthquake map of this week
Do you know how many earthquakes happened in/near the US just for the last week?
Individual earthquakes are displayed as markers while states are colored by their relative earthquake dangerousity - with deeper red means higher danger.
Zoom out and hover on the markers to explore when these quakes happened and their magnitudes. (Only earthquakes > M2.5 are displayed)
Click on states to learn how many earthquakes have occured there historically.
Red = Quakes stronger than M5.0
Orange = Quakes between M4.0 and M5.0
Yellow = Quakes weaker than M4.0
